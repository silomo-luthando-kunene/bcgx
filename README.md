**The Challenge:** PowerCo, a major energy utility, faced rising customer attrition in its SME segment. The business hypothesis was that churn was driven by price sensitivity, but a multi-dimensional analysis was required to validate this and identify actionable retention levers.

**The Solution:** I developed a comprehensive data science pipeline to analyze 14,000+ customer records. This involved identifying the drivers of churn through exploratory data analysis (EDA), engineering complex behavioural features, and deploying a Random Forest Classifier to predict churn risk with high precision.

**The Impact:** (High Predictive Accuracy) Developed a model with a 90% Overall Accuracy and a 0.85 ROC-AUC score, effectively separating loyal customers from high-risk churners.

* **Revenue Protection:** Identified that targeting the top 15% of high-risk customers could preserve significant net margins.
* **Strategic Shift:** Moved PowerCo from reactive discounting to proactive risk mitigation, optimizing marketing ROI and customer lifetime value.
